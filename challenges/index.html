Challenges

Are old-fashioned SS ranks on DMD too boring for you? Consider giving the challenges below a shot! All challenges require you to get an SS rank on DMD with Dante, with additional constraints that can be either in the form of forbidding certain actions (handicapped challenges) or requiring you to do additional battles (other challenges).

The rules for each challenge is clearly stated in the "description" column. Click the "Code" column link for each challenge to view the entries for players who have successfully completed the challenge.
